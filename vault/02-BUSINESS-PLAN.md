# MedBill Hacker — Business Plan

## Market Analysis
- **TAM:** $4.1 Trillion US healthcare spending; $140B in medical debt
- **SAM:** 100M Americans with medical debt or recent hospital bills
- **SOM Year 1:** 6,000 users (500/month) = $294,000 ARR

## Revenue Model
- **$49 flat fee** per audit + dispute letter generation
- **Unit Economics:** Revenue $49, Stripe fees $1.72, LLM cost $0.50, **Gross margin: $46.78 (95.5%)**

## Competitors
| Competitor | Fee | Weakness |
|-----------|-----|----------|
| Medical Billing Advocates | $200-500/hr | Too expensive for most patients |
| Dollar For (nonprofit) | Free | Slow, limited capacity, income-restricted |
| Resolve Medical Bills | 30% of savings | Expensive for large bills |

## Moat
- **$49 vs $200/hr** — 75x cheaper than human advocates
- **Instant results** — No waiting weeks for a human to review
- **CMS data integration** — Real Medicare rates by zip code for accurate benchmarking

## Go-to-Market
1. **SEO:** "how to dispute hospital bill", "medical bill too high"
2. **Reddit:** r/personalfinance, r/healthinsurance, r/medical_advice
3. **TikTok:** "I reduced my $4,500 hospital bill to $1,200 using AI"
