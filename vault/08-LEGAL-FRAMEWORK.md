| Fair Debt Collection Practices Act | 15 U.S.C. § 1692g | Right to dispute and demand itemization |
| Hospital Price Transparency Rule | 45 CFR § 180 | Hospitals must publish negotiated rates |

## Key CPT Code Knowledge
- Level 1 (99281): Minor, self-limited → ~$150
- Level 5 (99285): Life-threatening emergency → ~$1,200
- **Common fraud:** Coding a Level 2 visit as Level 5 to inflate revenue
