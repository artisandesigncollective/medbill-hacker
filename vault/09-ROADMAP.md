# MedBill Hacker — Roadmap

## Phase 1 ✅ MVP
- Landing page, bill scanner UI, Stripe checkout, dispute letter generator

## Phase 2: Real AI
- OCR pipeline (Tesseract/Google Vision), CMS Medicare database, line-by-line comparison

## Phase 3: Growth
- Auto-fax to hospitals via Lob, insurance appeal variant, user accounts

## Phase 4: Scale
- Mobile app, B2B for employer benefits programs, partnership with patient advocacy orgs
